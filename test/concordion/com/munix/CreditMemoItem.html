Credit Memo Item

The Credit Memo Module gives the user the ability to make adjustments (positive or negative) on an invoice, specifically a Sales Delivery. This is usually necessary if there are returns, changes in price or reports of discrepancy between the invoice and actual delivered quantity.

Credit/Debit Calculation

The final Credit or Debit Amount is dependent on the changes with Quantity or Price.
